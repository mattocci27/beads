Abundance_ of ith species at site j:

\[ Abundance_{ij} \sim Pois(\lambda_{ij}) \\ \lambda_{ij} = \beta_{0j} + \beta_{1j} \times Ridge_i + \beta_{2j} \times Forest_i + \beta_{3j} \times Ridge_i \times Forest_i \\ \beta_{mj} = \gamma_{m0} + \gamma_{m1} \times logLMA_j + \gamma_{m2} \times logWD_j + \epsilon_{mj} \\ \epsilon_{mj} \sim N(0, \sigma_m) \]
